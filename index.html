Mission Statement

The Wright Family Foundation aims to empower generations through the power of education. Founded in 2024, the foundation works with school districts in Massachusetts to provide educational scholarships to further the interests of students in a variety of fields. Each year, we aim to award scholarships to students who exemplify the values and qualities of the individuals for whom the scholarships are named.

The Marion D. Howard Nursing Scholarship

The Marion D. Howard Scholarship honors the legacy of Marion Howard, a dedicated nurse, educator, and community leader, who made significant contributions to nursing and giving back to her community.
